San Francisco: the best and the worst

Inner Richmond

Along the growing popularity of entertainment of Clement St., everyone comes together every night of the week for events held at the local bars and venues like Neck of the Woods and Richmond Republic. You can walk from Arguello down to Park Presidio and enjoy so many types of foods and markets, and everything is in a short walking distance from each other! It is a block over from Geary Blvd and public transit reaches the evergrowing events of the Inner Richmond.

South of Mission/Geneva Ave.

Though there are many areas of the city that, if not aware of the past few years, can be more "dangerous" than others, Geneva Ave. has been a battle of broken streets, home invasions and gun-point robbery. Even with light out, the area is known for unneccessary confrontations with muggers and gang members. Though residents of the area continue to thrive there, it isn't a place I'd be any time of day.

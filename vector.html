Bunnier Goes Fishing

This is Bunnier [pronounced: Bun-neay]. He loves his mornings because that’s when the best carrots are swimming upstream and taste the sweetest. I’ve never worked on vector graphics with a raster image set to the scene before so it was great seeing how the 2 came together.
